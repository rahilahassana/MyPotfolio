VBTC- Virtual Bot

A virtual mining and trading platform where users can explore, mine, and grow their digital assets in an interactive metaverse environment.

About the Project

VBTC VirtualBot is a blockchain-based virtual mining simulator that combines gameplay with real-world crypto mechanics. Players can mine tokens, trade, and interact in real time through integrated wallet connectivity and smart contract support. The game features core multiplayer systems built with Photon Networking, along with voice and text chat for seamless communication, delivering an optimized, low-latency experience that bridges gaming and blockchain technology.

Key features include user-owned avatars, interactive multiplayer mini-games, an NFT marketplace, virtual real estate, and token-based governance. MelonVerse empowers players to truly own their digital journey.
